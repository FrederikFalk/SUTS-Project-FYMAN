Philadelphia Cheesecake Parfaits

Ingredients Directions

2 pkg. (8 oz. each) Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened
1/4 cup sugar
2 Tbsp. milk
1 tsp. zest and 1 Tbsp. juice from 1 lemon
1-1/2 cups thawed COOL WHIP Whipped Topping
15 square shortbread cookies, crushed
4 cups strawberries, quartered
1 cup blueberries

Beat cream cheese,
sugar,
milk,
lemon zest
and juice in medium bowl with mixer until blended.
Gently stir in COOL WHIP.
Reserve 3 Tbsp. cookie crumbs.
1 Tbsp. of the remaining cookie crumbs
into each of 10 parfait glasses;
top with 2 Tbsp. cream cheese mixture.
Cover with layers of strawberries,
remaining cookie crumbs,
remaining cream cheese mixture
and blueberries.
Sprinkle with reserved cookie crumbs.
